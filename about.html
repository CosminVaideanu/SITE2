Cosmin Vaideanu

Interested in science, climate and protecting the planet.

Starting from 2015 I am a Phd student at the Faculty of Physics (Bucharest University, Romania), under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Mihai Dima and fully expect to complete my PhD degree requirements by summer 2018. Also at the University of Bucharest, between 2012 and 2016, I graduated 2 masters programs in Environmental Science and Atmospheric Physics.

In the context of global warming and with an increasingly overpopulated planet, climate variability has become a central subject of discussion for the scientific and politic community alike. In my research, using statistical methods on both observed and model-generated data, I Investigate natural and anthropogenic variability, from regional to global scale, through the physical mechanisms associated with climate modes.
